Echeveria

The necklaces are created with Arabic thread weaved around silver rings that measure 3 centimetres in diameter.

To ensure its longevity, protect the necklace from moisture - take it off before bathing, especially avoiding contact with detergents. When the threads are wet, they become especially prone to damage, and detergents such as shower gel or hair conditioner can block the treads' fibres and prevent them from drying, causing them to lose their original shape. We understand that the necklace might require cleaning sometimes - in this case, mix laundry detergent into warm water and shake the necklace in the mixture while holding it by the pendant bail. Dry well and avoid washing your jewellery too often - similarly to textiles, the more we wash them, the more colour and texture they lose.
