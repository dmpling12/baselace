OUR STORY


Dorota, the founder and owner of Base Lace, has always wanted to create something. Many ideas came and went during the years until a birthday gift idea sparked something special in June 2019. Prototypes only increased the eagerness since the potential available to uncover became clear.

A business plan, a bunch of collaborations and hours spent on research and formalities paid off in the form of a brand that unconventionally wraps the traditional Koniakow lace with craft, patience and precision to create a product which brings luxury and beauty to every recipient. All for you, from a girl from the Beskidy mountains, with components outsourced from Polish companies as a sign of solidarity and support.

The beautiful Silesian Beskids are home to the lace traditions that inspired the creation of Base Lace.



OUR MISSION


Base Lace is here to bring beauty in the form of luxurious craft into the lives of its customers.
